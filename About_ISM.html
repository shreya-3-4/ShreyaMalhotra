About ISM

Independent Study and Mentorship (ISM) is a challenging and dynamic elective for high-achieving juniors and seniors at Frisco ISD high schools. Students must undergo a competitive application and interview process to enroll in the course. Once accepted, participants dive into an immersive learning experience focused on their chosen field of interest.
